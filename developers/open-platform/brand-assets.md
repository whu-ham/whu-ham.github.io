This page provides Ham brand icon resources that you can use directly in your application to display the Ham login entry or brand identity.

::: tip Usage Guidelines

* Use **64×64** or **128×128** for button icons
* Use **256×256** or larger for logos on display pages
* Do not stretch, crop, or modify the icon's colors or proportions
  :::

## Usage Guidelines

**Do:**

* Keep the icon's original aspect ratio — avoid stretching or compressing
* Maintain adequate whitespace around the icon
* Ensure sufficient contrast when placing on dark backgrounds

**Don't:**

* Modify the icon's colors
* Overlay text or other elements on the icon
* Use the icon to imply official Ham endorsement or certification
