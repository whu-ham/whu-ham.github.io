# User Center

After logging in to your Ham account, you can manage your account information and settings in the User Center.

## Ham Account

Ham supports the following login methods:

* Passkey
* QQ
* WeChat
* GitHub
* Ziqiang Auth
* Apple Sign-In (iOS only)

## Profile

After logging in, you can change your avatar and nickname in the User Center.

## Login Devices

You can view the list of currently logged-in devices and remove any devices you no longer use.

## Social Accounts

You can view your linked social accounts, link new ones, or unlink existing ones. The supported social account types are the same as the login methods.

## Passkey Management

You can manage your Passkeys here, including adding new Passkeys or removing existing ones. Passkey is a passwordless login method that uses your device's biometric authentication (such as Face ID or fingerprint) for identity verification.

## QR Code Login

You can use the app to scan a login QR code on Ham Web to quickly log in.

## Authorized Apps

You can view and manage third-party apps that have been authorized to log in with your Ham account. If you no longer use an app, you can revoke its authorization.

## More

In "More," you can perform actions such as account deactivation.
