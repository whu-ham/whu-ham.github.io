# Luojia E-Card

::: tip Version requirements
iOS: 1.3.2 (build >= 96)

Android: 1.4.3
:::

You can use the Luojia E-Card with Ham.

## Preparation

Before using it, log in to your information portal account in "Me" -> "Information Portal Settings."

## Shortcut

Long press the Ham icon on your home screen to open the Luojia E-Card quickly.
