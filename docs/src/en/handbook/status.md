# Status
The status page shows the tasks you need to handle today.

### Weather
Shows real-time weather so you can adjust what you wear.

### Library Reservation
Shows your current reservations. You can also change the reservation time quickly.

### Classes
Shows your courses for today or tomorrow.

### Schedule
Shows the schedule items you added in Ham.

### Shuttle
Shows campus shuttle info for the nearest stop.

### Sports
Shows your booked sports venues.
