# Sync
With sync, you can:
- Log in to the same library account on multiple devices without conflicts.
- More features coming soon.

Sync will be available at the right time. You can also check the latest updates in "Me" -> "Bulletin."

## Ham account
You must log in to a Ham account to use sync. Ham supports QQ, WeChat, Github, Ziqiang Auth, and Apple (iOS) logins.

After logging in, you can view logged-in devices or linked social accounts in your profile. You can also remove devices or unlink accounts.

## Required permissions
- Notifications - for sync status
- Network - to connect to Ham servers and relay to other devices
- Third-party login - for identity verification
